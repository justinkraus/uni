Efficient R&D

Comparing University Research and Development Output with Spending

Research and Development

Research and development (R&D) is a competitive feature of modern universities. Better R&D leads to improved reputation and connections with private industry.

R&D is shown on the axes as the number of patents and quality of the research program (influential citations in highly regarded scientific journals). Bubble colors correspond to the national rank of the University.

Spending

Spending is reflected in the size of the bubbles. Click the button to flip how the spending scale is reflected.
